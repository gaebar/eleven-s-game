Instructions

Control Eleven with your keyboard arrow keys and save humanity from the monsters of the Upside Down World.

To save humanity from the Demogorgon, follow the pumpkin seeds to reach the Eggos. Once you eat an Eggo, all the Demogorgon turn in a vulnerable mode, and you can make them disappear.

You have three lives for each level to accomplish your mission.
