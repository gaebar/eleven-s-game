Instructions

Control Eleven with your keyboard arrow keys and save her from the monsters of the Upside Down World. You have three lives for each level.

To save her from the Demogorgon, follow the pumpkin seeds to reach the Eggos.

Once you eat an Eggo, all the Demogorgon turn in a vulnerable mode, and you can make them disappear.
